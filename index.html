Yes! I always confuse 2NF and 3NF
EduMind AI
Classic confusion! Here's the key difference:

2NF: No partial dependency (non-key attribute depends on PART of composite key)
3NF: No transitive dependency (non-key attribute depends on another non-key attribute)

Example: Student(RollNo, SubjectID, StudentName, TeacherName)
- TeacherName depends on SubjectID (not the whole key) → 2NF violation
- Fix: Split into Student(RollNo, SubjectID) + Subject(SubjectID, TeacherName)
